Educational services

classroom. Kits are expensive to keep up to date with extensive work, and teachers generally lack technical skills.
Therefore, the IRA has developed a comprehensive set of services tailored to the needs of each institution
to facilitate the seamless integration of STEM & Robotics into existing curricula.
